Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

Who we are

SB Launcher is an independent desktop companion for Roblox. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation.

What we store

  • Roblox OAuth access and refresh tokens, encrypted on the local API
  • SB Launcher session identifiers
  • Optional favorites, launch history, theme presets, and outfit presets
  • Local preferences such as visuals and graphics settings
  • Optional public profile cosmetics synced through our Cloudflare Worker

What we do not do

  • We do not ask for or store your Roblox password
  • We do not sell personal data
  • We do not require an account separate from Roblox OAuth

Cloud services

If cloud features are enabled, your Roblox user id, display name, and public cosmetics may be stored so other SB Launcher users can see them. Update checks use a public version manifest.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be opened as an issue on the project GitHub repository.
