Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Who we are

SB Launcher is an independent desktop companion for Roblox. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation.

What we store

  • Roblox OAuth access and refresh tokens, encrypted on the local API
  • SB Launcher session identifiers
  • Optional favorites, launch history, theme presets, and outfit presets
  • Local preferences such as visuals and graphics settings

What we do not store

  • Roblox passwords
  • .ROBLOSECURITY cookies
  • Payment or billing card data

How data is used

Data is used only to sign you in, load your Roblox profile/friends/avatar information you request, and remember launcher preferences. We do not sell personal data.

Where data lives

On the Windows desktop app, data is kept locally under %LOCALAPPDATA%\SB Launcher on your machine. Hosted API deployments store only what is required for that deployment’s session and preference features.

Contact

For privacy questions about SB Launcher, open an issue on the project’s GitHub repository.
